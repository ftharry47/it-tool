Quick Help

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen or submit a ticket with Issue Type "Password reset". For urgent access, call the IT Helpdesk for priority assistance.

My computer is running slow - what should I do?

Try restarting your computer first. Close unnecessary applications and browser tabs. If the issue persists, submit a ticket with "Laptop / desktop issue" as the issue type.

I can't connect to VPN - help?

Check your internet connection first. Restart the VPN client and try connecting again. Make sure your credentials are correct. If issues persist, submit a ticket under "VPN issue".

How do I report a security concern?

Report any suspected security breach immediately. Select "Phishing / suspicious email" or "Malware concern" as the issue type. Do not share details via unsecured email.

Critical / System Outage?

For system outages or critical issues affecting multiple users, call the IT Helpdesk directly at (123) 456-7890. Available 24/7 for emergencies.

HIPAA Reminder

Do not include patient names, MRNs, or other Protected Health Information (PHI) in your ticket description. Use general terms to describe the issue.

IT Support Terms and Conditions

Last updated: January 2025

1. Scope of Support

The IT Support Portal provides technical assistance for systems, applications, and IT infrastructure. Support is available during business hours with 24/7 on-call support for critical systems.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and complete information when submitting requests
  • Do NOT include patient PHI (names, MRNs, SSN, DOB) in ticket descriptions
  • Respond promptly to IT team communications
  • Follow security policies and acceptable use guidelines
  • Report security incidents or potential breaches immediately

3. Response Times

Response times are based on ticket priority:

  • P1 (Critical): 15-30 minutes
  • P2 (High): 1-2 hours
  • P3 (Medium): 4 hours
  • P4 (Low): 24 hours

4. Data Privacy

All IT support activities comply with data protection regulations. Support tickets are stored securely with access limited to authorized IT personnel.
